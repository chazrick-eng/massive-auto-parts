Who we are

The North Coast's Parts & Car Source

Massive Auto Parts & Car Sales — also known as Massive Used Parts — sits on Main Street in Pineapple, Ocho Rios Bay. We bring auto parts and car sales together in one place, so drivers across St. Ann and the north coast have a single, reliable stop.

As an authorized dealer in used Japanese engines and body parts, we specialise in the hard-to-find: the right engine, the right panel, the part the next shop says they can't get. New or used, we carry deep inventory for Toyota, Honda, Nissan, Mazda and more — and we source what we don't stock.
